FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Do I need to know coding to use Pagefolio?
+

Not at all. Pagefolio is built for everyone. Just fill in your details and your page is ready. No technical knowledge required.

Can I use my own domain name?
+

Yes! On our Pro and Business plans, you can connect your own domain (like yourname.in) to your Pagefolio profile. We provide step-by-step DNS setup instructions.

Is my page really free? What's the catch?
+

Yes, free means free. The Free plan gives you a real, live profile page at ablabsbusiness-create.github.io/Pagefolio/yourname with no time limit. You only need to upgrade if you want premium features like analytics, more links, or a custom domain.

Can I change my username later?
+

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days. We recommend choosing something clean and professional from the start — your old URL will redirect to your new one for 7 days.

Is Pagefolio available in Hindi?
+

The platform currently operates in English, but you can add content in any language including Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, etc. Multi-language dashboard support is coming soon.

What payment methods are accepted?
+

We accept all major payment methods — UPI, Razorpay, debit/credit cards, and net banking. All payments are in Indian Rupees (₹).
