Legal Practice Areas

Criminal Defence

Civil Litigation

Property & Real Estate

Corporate Law

Banking & Finance

Domestic Violence

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions clients usually ask before booking a legal consultation.

Do I need to book an appointment before visiting your office?
Yes, booking in advance is recommended so we can give your matter proper time and review the initial details before your meeting.
What should I bring to the first consultation?
Please carry any notices, FIR copy, court papers, agreements, identity proof, and a short summary of the issue so the discussion can be more productive.
Do you handle urgent criminal and bail matters?
Yes, urgent criminal matters including bail-related guidance can be reviewed on priority depending on the facts, documents, and court schedule.
Can I get legal advice online or over WhatsApp first?
Yes, you can share basic details through WhatsApp or call first, and then a proper consultation can be scheduled for detailed legal advice.
Will my case details remain confidential?
Yes, all client communications and case discussions are handled with professional confidentiality and discretion.
