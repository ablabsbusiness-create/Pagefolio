Meet Dr. Aarav Sharma, a pediatrician who truly listens

With over 15 years of dedicated pediatric practice, Dr. Aarav Sharma has become one of Jaipur’s most trusted child specialists.

"Every child is unique. My goal is to provide personalized care that not only treats illness but supports lifelong health and wellbeing."

Dr. Sharma completed his MD in Pediatrics from SMS Medical College, Jaipur, followed by a fellowship in Neonatology. He has served at leading hospitals across Rajasthan and is known for his calm communication, clinical judgment, and parent-friendly guidance.
